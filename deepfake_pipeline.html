── STEP 1 — PREPROCESSING ──
⚙️ Audio Preprocessing
→ Εξαγωγή audio από MP4 torchaudio
→ Μετατροπή σε mono (mean channels)
→ Resample 16 kHz
→ Trim silence librosa top_db=20
→ Normalize amplitude [-1, 1]
→ Output: tensor (1, T)
⚙️ Video Preprocessing
→ Εξαγωγή frames OpenCV VideoCapture
→ Uniform sampling max 100 frames
→ BGR → RGB
→ Output: list[np.ndarray (H, W, 3)]
